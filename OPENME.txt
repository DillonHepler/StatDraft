Stat Draft — iOS (SwiftUI)

Open on a Mac with Xcode 15+:
  Double-click StatDraft.xcodeproj
  Select an iPhone simulator (iOS 17+), press Run.

Set your Team in Signing & Capabilities for a physical device.

This app uses bundled demo_stats.json generated from nflverse player stats.
Regenerate locally:
  mkdir -p data
  curl -L "https://github.com/nflverse/nflverse-data/releases/download/player_stats/player_stats.parquet" -o data/player_stats.parquet
  python3 scripts/build_stats_db.py
  python3 scripts/validate_stats_db.py

Coverage reality note:
  Single-season + single-position buckets do not have 300+ unique players in real data.
  The current database includes all available players for the prompt year/position buckets.

Multiplayer in v1: same device, pass-and-play (snake draft). Real-time online sync is not included yet.
