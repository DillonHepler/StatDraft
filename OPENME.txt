Stat Draft — iOS (SwiftUI)

Open on a Mac with Xcode 15+:
  Double-click StatDraft.xcodeproj
  Select an iPhone simulator (iOS 17+), press Run.

Set your Team in Signing & Capabilities for a physical device.

This MVP uses bundled demo_stats.json (classic-era NFL stars, approximate stats for gameplay).
Replace StatsRepository’s data source later with a licensed stats API for production.

Multiplayer in v1: same device, pass-and-play (snake draft). Real-time online sync is not included yet.
